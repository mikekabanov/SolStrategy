## 📌 Strategy Overview

### Entry Signals
- Generated by Gradient Boosting ML model
- Probability threshold: 0.5 (50%)
- Based on technical indicators
- ~3.3% of candles generate signals

### Position Management
- Size: $1,000 per trade
- Capital: $10,000 initial
- Max concurrent: 10 positions
- Position limit improves diversification

### Exit Rules
**Either condition triggers exit**:
1. **Profit target**: +3% gain reached
2. **Timeout**: 120 minutes elapsed

### Risk Controls
- Hard stop-loss for catastrophic loss
- Position sizing limits daily loss
- Correlation control between positions
- Drawdown monitoring active
